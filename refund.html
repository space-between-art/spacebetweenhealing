Our Commitment: Revisions until you're satisfied. In most cases, we resolve issues through revisions, not refunds.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be requested when:

  • Project has not started (within 48 hours of deposit)
  • We cannot deliver on time due to our fault and no solution can be reached
  • Deliverables significantly differ from the confirmed quotation

2. Refund Rates

Project StageRefund Rate
Within 48 hours of deposit (work not started)100% of deposit
Design in progress (before first draft)50% of deposit
First draft submittedNo refund
Project completed and deliveredNo refund

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Client cancels for personal reasons
  • Delays caused by client not providing required materials
  • Change in design style preference (resolved through revisions)
  • Project completed with all files delivered

4. Refund Process

  • Email hello@spacebetweenhealing.com to request refund
  • Include reason and order details
  • We respond within 5 business days
  • If approved, refund processed within 7-14 business days

5. Refund Method

Refunds are returned via original payment method. For Wise or international payments, transaction fees may apply and are borne by the client.
